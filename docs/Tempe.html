Tempe's Hidden Gems

Sunbar Tempe

Visit Sunbar Tempe for lunch and one of their signature cocktails!

GuacStar

Check out GuacStar, a Mexican inspired Plant-Based Restaurant and Cantina

Four Peaks

Defining craft beer of the Southwest since '96, Four Peaks never disappoints!

House of Tricks

Visit House of Tricks, a Tempe staple for over 30 years with their cozy outdoor ambiance, and signature seasonal menu

Casey Moore's

Hit Casey Moore's Oyster House for seafood, pub grub and a night of beer and cocktails with friends, this Tempe staple is a must try!

Alter Ego

Located at the Canopy hotel in Downtown Tempe, Alter Ego provides modern-american fusion cuisine and a must try signature cocktail menu/p>

Doc and Eddys

Grab some wings, and catch the game at this go-to local favorite sports bar, Doc and Eddys!

Fate Brewing Company

With three locations in the valley this local brewpub is the perfect place for lunch on the patio!

Yucca Tap Room

Tempe fixture with live music, a huge beer and whiskey selecion, newly added patio area, and a distinctly unique bar food menu

The Porch

The Porch is an upscale yet relaxed restaurant with three current locations in Arcadia, Gilbert, and Tempe. Check them out for their lively atmosphere and great food and drink menu.

Tempe Tavern

This rustic-style watering hole in a 1918 dairy barn features a classic pub menu & sports on TV.

Pedal Haus Brewery

Pedal Haus Brewery offers elevated gastro-pub grub, award-winning craft beer and a unique atmosphere that is engaging yet relaxing.
