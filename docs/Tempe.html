Sunbar Tempe

Defining craft beer of the Southwest since '96, Four Peaks never disappoints!

House of Tricks

Visit House of Tricks, a Tempe staple for over 30 years with their cozy outdoor ambiance, and signature seasonal menu

Fate Brewing Company

With three locations in the valley this local brewpub is the perfect place for lunch on the patio!

Tempe fixture with live music, a huge beer and whiskey selecion, newly added patio area, and a distinctly unique bar food menu
