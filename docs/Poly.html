Polytechnic

About Polytech:

Arizona State University (ASU), Polytechnic is a physical campus of ASU tucked away just east of Gilbert, located in Mesa, Arizona. There are many beautiful places to site see and stop by for a quick bite to eat. The Polytechnic campus was built where a former Air Force base used to stand, and still has a rural vibe to it. There are countless wild animals that can be spotted near and on the campus. The roadrunner is one of the most commonly seen animals, hopping from tree to tree or running through campus. There are many different eateries and areas to sight see which you would not expect from a campus that is for the most part forgotten. Overall the location of the area is truly beautiful and you will not run out of new places to visit when near ASU Polytech.

Burrito Shack

TLooks can be deceiving. The Burrito Shack is located less than a mile away from ASU’s Polytechnic Campus. It is literally a shack that serves all different kinds of burritos.

Surf City Sandwich

Surf City Sandwich is a brand-new sub restaurant located less than a mile away from ASU’s Polytechnic Campus. There are a variety of subs that are delicious. There are a lot of different options, including warm and cold subs, as well as vegetarian options.

Joe’s Farm Grill

Joe’s Farm Grill is located about 3 miles away from the ASU’s Polytechnic Campus. It is a quaint little restaurant surround by a beautiful orchard. There are orange trees and other fruits and vegetables growing on site. It is a lovely place to visit and eat outside on the picnic tables for a quick meal.
